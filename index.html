About Us

Avanti Afrika Tours and Safaris is a premier tour operator based in Cape Town, South Africa. With over a decade of experience, we have been providing exceptional travel experiences since our establishment in 2008. Our small team of knowledgeable and registered tour guides are dedicated to creating unforgettable adventures across the diverse landscapes of South Africa. From thrilling wildlife safaris to cultural immersions, we offer a wide range of tailor-made tours that cater to the unique preferences and interests of our clients. Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and personalized service sets us apart as a trusted and reliable choice for exploring the wonders of South Africa.

Team Avanti Afrika

George Alexander is an experienced and fully registered tour guide from Cape Town, South Africa. As a born Capetonian, he has a deep understanding of the city's history, culture, and attractions. With his friendly and engaging personality, George ensures that every tour is informative, entertaining, and memorable. He takes pride in showcasing the diverse landscapes, iconic landmarks, and hidden gems of Cape Town to visitors from around the world.

Paul Avenant plays a crucial role in connecting travellers with the best options for their visit to Cape Town, South Africa. As our virtual guest relations officer, he collaborates closely with each client to understand their preferences, budget, and desired itinerary, offering personalized recommendations and suggestions. Having guided tours in Cape Town since 2008, Paul utilizes his extensive knowledge of the Western Cape to curate unique and tailored travel experiences that cater to individual interests and requirements.
