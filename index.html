About Us

Avanti Afrika Tours and Safaris is a premier tour operator based in Cape Town, South Africa. With over a decade of experience, we have been providing exceptional travel experiences since our establishment in 2008. Our team of knowledgeable and registered tour guides are dedicated to creating unforgettable adventures across the diverse landscapes of South Africa. From thrilling wildlife safaris to cultural immersions, we offer a wide range of tailor-made tours that cater to the unique preferences and interests of our clients. Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and personalized service sets us apart as a trusted and reliable choice for exploring the wonders of South Africa.
